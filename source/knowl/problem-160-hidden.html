Find the least upper bound (supremum) and greatest lower bound (infimum) of the following sets of real numbers, if they exist. (If one does not exist then say so.)

  1. \(\displaystyle S=\{\frac{1}{n}\,|\,n=1,2,3,\ldots\}\)

  2. \(T=\{r\,|\,r\) is rational and \(r^2\lt 2\}\)

  3. \(\displaystyle (-\infty,0)\cup(1,\infty)\)

  4. \(\displaystyle R=\{\frac{(-1)^n}{n}\,|\,n=1,2,3,\ldots\}\)

  5. \(\displaystyle (2,3\pi]\cap\QQ\)

  6. The empty set \(\emptyset\)
