Problem 8.1.5.c shows that the Taylor series of \(e^x\) expanded at zero converges to \(e^x\) for any real number \(x\text{.}\) Theorem 8.1.1 can be used in a similar fashion to show that

\begin{equation*} e^x=\sum_{n=0}^\infty\frac{e^a(x-a)^n}{n!} \end{equation*}

for any real numbers \(a\) and \(x\text{.}\)
