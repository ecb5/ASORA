Similarly you use the symbol \(\NN\) to denote the set of positive integers. With your new word and symbol you make the statement “the cardinality (number of elements) of \(\NN\) is \(\aleph_0\text{.}\)” This statement has the same grammatical form as the statement “the number of elements (cardinality) of \(S\) is three.” Since three is a number you conclude that \(\aleph_0\) is also a number.
