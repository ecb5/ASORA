Thus we choose \((0, 0, 0, \ldots)\) to represent \(0^*\) and any element of \(x\text{,}\) say \((x_1, x_2, \ldots)\text{,}\) to represent \(x\text{.}\) Then

\begin{align*} (0, 0, 0, \ldots) + (x_1, x_2, x_3, \ldots) \amp = (x_1, x_2, x_3, \ldots)\\ \amp = x\text{.} \end{align*}
