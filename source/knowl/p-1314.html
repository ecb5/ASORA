By Corollary 10.4.5 of Chapter 10, we know that a bounded increasing sequence such as \((a_n)\) converges, say to \(c\text{.}\) Since \(a_n\leq a_m\leq b_n\) for \(m>n\) and \(\limit{m}{\infty}{a_m}=c\text{,}\) then for any fixed \(n\text{,}\) \(a_n\leq c\leq b_n\text{.}\) This says \(c\in\left[a_n, b_n\right]\) for all \(n\in\NN\text{.}\)
