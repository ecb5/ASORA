Section 5.3 Additional Problems

Problem 5.3.1.

Use Taylor's formula to find the Taylor series of the given function expanded about the given point \(a\text{.}\)

(a)

\(f(x)=\ln\left(1+x\right)\text{,}\) \(a=0\)

(b)

\(f(x)=e^x\text{,}\) \(a=-1\)

(c)

\(f(x)=x^3+x^2+x+1\text{,}\) \(a=0\)

(d)

\(f(x)=x^3+x^2+x+1\text{,}\) \(a=1\)
