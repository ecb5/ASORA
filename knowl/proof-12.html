Proof

Let \(\eps>0\text{.}\) Let \(\delta=\sqrt{\eps}\text{.}\) If \(|\,x-0|\lt \delta\text{,}\) then \(|\,x|\lt \sqrt{\eps}\text{.}\) Thus
\begin{equation*} \abs{x^2-0^2}=|\,x|^2\lt \left(\sqrt{\eps}\right)^2=\eps\text{.} \end{equation*}
Thus by the definition, \(f\) is continuous at \(0\text{.}\)
