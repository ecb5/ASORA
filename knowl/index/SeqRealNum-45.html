Problem 6.1.14.

(a)

Provide a rigorous definition for \(\limit{n}{\infty}{s_n}\neq s\text{.}\)

(b)

Use your definition to show that for any real number \(a\text{,}\) \(\limit{n}{\infty}{\left(\left(-1\right)^n\right)}\neq a\text{.}\)
Hint.
Choose \(\eps=1\) and use the fact that \(\Big|a-(-1)^n\Big|\lt 1\) is equivalent to \(\left(-1\right)^n-1\lt a\lt \left(-1\right)^n+1\) to show that no choice of \(N\) will work for this \(\eps\text{.}\)
