Problem 6.1.11.

Show that when \(x=\frac{1}{4}\)
\begin{equation*} 4\sum_{k=0}^\infty\left(-1\right)^{k+1} \sin\left(\left(2k+1\right)\pi x\right)=4\left(-\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}- \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}-\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}+\cdots\right)\text{.} \end{equation*}
