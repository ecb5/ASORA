Problem 4.1.6. Cauchy’s Counterexample, Part 2.

(a)

Let \(m\) be a nonnegative integer. Show that \(\limit{y}{\pm\infty}{\frac{y^m}{e^{y^2}}}=0\text{.}\)
Hint.
Induction and a dash of L’Hôpital’s rule should do the trick.

(b)

Prove that \(\limit{y}{\pm\infty}{\frac{q(y)}{e^{y^2}}}=0\) for any polynomial \(q\text{.}\)

(c)

Let \(f(x)\) be as in equation (16) and show that for every nonnegative integer \(n\text{,}\) \(f^{(n)}(0)=0\text{.}\)
