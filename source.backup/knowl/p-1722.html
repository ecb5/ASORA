Let \(\beta\) be a cut, \(y\) and \(z\) be positive rational numbers not in \(\beta\) with \(y\lt z\text{,}\) and let \(\eps>0\) be any rational number. Then there exist positive rational numbers \(r\) and \(s\) with \(r\in\beta\text{,}\) and \(s\not\in\beta\text{,}\) such that \(s\lt z\text{,}\) and \(s-r\lt \eps\text{.}\)
