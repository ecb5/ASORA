Suppose \(a\neq0\text{.}\) If

\begin{align} ax^2+bx+c =0\tag{2.2.0.1}\\ \end{align}

then

\begin{align} x^2+\frac{b}{a}x\amp =-\frac{c}{a}\tag{2.2.0.2}\\ \end{align}

Now let \(x=y-\frac{b}{2a}\) giving

\begin{align} y^2\amp = -\frac{c}{a} +\frac{b^2}{4a^2}\tag{2.2.0.3}\\ y\amp = \frac{\pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}\tag{2.2.0.4}\\ x\amp = \frac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}\tag{2.2.0.5} \end{align}
