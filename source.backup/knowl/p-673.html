Note that for all \(t\in[\,a,x]\text{,}\) we have \(m\leq f^{(n+1)}(t)\leq M\text{.}\) Since \(x-t\geq 0\text{,}\) this gives us

\begin{equation} m\left(x-t\right)^n\leq f^{(n+1)}(t)(x-t)^n\leq M(x-t)^n\tag{8.2.1} \end{equation}

and so

\begin{equation} \int_{t=a}^xm\left(x-t\right)^n\dx{ t}\leq\int_{t=a}^xf^{(n+1)}(t)(x-t)^n\dx{ t}\leq \int_{t=a}^xM(x-t)^n\dx{ t}\text{.}\tag{8.2.2} \end{equation}
